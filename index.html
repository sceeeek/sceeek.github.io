Guidance

In this webpage, you can check the score of each participant and try to form the best group based on one or multiple skills selected by you. The filtering functionality in this web page allows you to interactively filter and display specific data based on their preferences or criteria. After you choose the range of certain skills, the 'Possible Group Members' will show the corresponding names based on your choice. By clicking on these names, you can add them to your selected group.
